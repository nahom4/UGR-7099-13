ETHIOPIA A PLACE OF WONDERS

Lalibela

There’s something intensely wonderful and spiritual about Lalibela, a truly amazing town located in the northern region of Ethiopia. This small village is a World Heritage site – acclaimed for its rock-hewn churches, stunning rural landscape, and devout Christianity. A visit to Lalibela is life-changing, leaving you with a feeling of stepping back into a culture that time forgot.

Axum

Axum is an ancient city located in north ethiopia. it has a rich history of over 3000 years and the artifacts to show for it. The magnificent oblisks this beautiful city houses are the main attraction of the city.

Harar

Harar a city over 500 years old had a major impact on the history of ethiopia. It was at the very center of the various power shifts that have happened 500 years ago. The gegol wall and it's ancient mosques are it's main attractions.
